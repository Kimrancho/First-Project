ZEN2 is the codename for the successor of AMD's Zen and Zen+ microarchitectures, fabricated on the 7 nanometer node from TSMC
and powering the third generation of Ryzen processors, known as Ryzen 3000 for the mainstream desktop chips and Threadripper 3000
for high-end desktop system The Ryzen 3000 series CPUs were released on July 7, 2019, while the Zen 2-based Epyc server CPUs
(codename "Rome") were released on August 7, 2019. An additional Ryzen 9 3950X is set to be released in September 2019.
